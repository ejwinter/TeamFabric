# Theresa Blount

Role: Director of Compliance
Organization: Riverdale Health System
Status: Active

## Summary

Theresa is the compliance gatekeeper for all PHI-adjacent data work at Riverdale Health System. She must review and sign off on any new EHR data feeds before they go to production. She is thorough and detail-oriented, and values being looped in early rather than at the end of a project.

## Areas of Interest

- HIPAA compliance and PHI data governance
- EHR data access controls and audit trails
- New data feed approvals

## Expertise

- Healthcare compliance regulations (HIPAA, state-level)
- Data governance frameworks
- Risk assessment for data access expansions

## Communication Preferences

Prefers email for formal sign-off requests; include a one-page summary with scope, PHI exposure, and controls in place. Does not need to be on recurring standups. Loop in early when a new EHR feed is being scoped, not after development is underway.
